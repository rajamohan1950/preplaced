About Preplaced

We're on a mission to democratize access to world-class mentorship

Our Mission

At Preplaced, we believe that everyone deserves access to world-class mentorship, regardless of their background or location. Our mission is to connect ambitious professionals with experienced mentors who can guide them through their career journey, helping them achieve their goals faster and more effectively.

Our Vision

We envision a world where career growth is not limited by geography, network, or resources. Through our platform, we aim to create a global community of mentors and mentees, fostering knowledge sharing, skill development, and career advancement for millions of professionals worldwide.

Our Story

Preplaced was founded with a simple observation: talented professionals often struggle to find the right guidance to advance their careers. Traditional mentorship is limited by geography, time constraints, and network access. We set out to solve this problem by creating a platform that makes world-class mentorship accessible to everyone.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of professionals achieve their career goals through our network of experienced mentors from top companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, and many more.
