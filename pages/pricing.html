Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your mentorship experience, we'll refund your payment.

How do I get matched with a mentor?

After signing up, you'll fill out a brief questionnaire about your career goals. Our team will match you with the best mentor based on your needs.

Are there any long-term commitments?

No, all our plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime without any penalties.
